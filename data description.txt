This dataset includes data on adult's diet, physical activity, and weight status from Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System.
This data is used for DNPAO's Data, Trends, and Maps database, which provides national and state specific data on obesity,
 nutrition, physical activity, and breastfeeding.
